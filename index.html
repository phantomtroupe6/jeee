    STEP 5 - COMPLETE THE THIRD LAYER CORNERS

    First, put the corners in the correct position (A). You may have 0, 1, or all corners in the correct position, either correctly or reversed. If one corner piece is correct, turn the cube so this correct corner is in the front top right position. The piece may not be turned the correct way around. Repeat the sequence until all the corners are in the correct position.
